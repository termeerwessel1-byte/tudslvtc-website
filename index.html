Who We Are

The United Dutch Slovak Logistics (TUDSL) brings together drivers under one logistics banner.

A friendly Dutch–Slovak community with active Discord communication.

Weekly convoys across Europe with organised routes and safe driving.

We focus on clean driving, proper behaviour and realistic logistics.

Rules

Respect, realism en teamwork staan centraal. Volg TruckersMP‑regels en rijd veilig.
